How to generate SSL Certificate?

Step 1: openssl genrsa -out server-key.pem 1024
Step 2: openssl req -new -key server-key.pem -out server-csr.pem
Step 3: openssl x509 -req -in server-csr.pem -signkey server-key.pem -out server-cert.pem
